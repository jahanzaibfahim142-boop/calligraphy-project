Thuluth Script

Often referred to as the most elegant Arabic script, Thuluth is characterized by its curving, interlocking strokes and grand vertical scale.

Naskh Script

Designed for supreme legibility, Naskh is the standard for Quranic texts and modern publishing due to its balanced proportions.

Kufic Script

The oldest calligraphic form, Kufic is highly geometric and angular. It was widely used in early Islamic architecture and manuscripts.
