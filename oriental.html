Kaishu (Standard)

Known as "Regular Script," this is the most formal and legible style. It requires high precision and square-like stability in every stroke.

Gyoushu (Walking)

A semi-cursive style where the brush flows from one character to the next. It represents a balance between speed and formal structure.

The Four Treasures

The foundation of the craft: the Brush, Inkstick, Rice Paper, and Inkstone. Mastery begins with understanding these essential materials.
